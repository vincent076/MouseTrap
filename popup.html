Mouse Trap

Clickbait is a term for web content whose main goal is to get users to click on a link to go to a certain webpage. Clickbait headlines typically aim to exploit the "curiosity gap", providing just enough information to make readers curious, but not enough to satisfy their curiosity without clicking through to the linked content. The techniques employed by the clickbait authors presented little or no legitimate well-researched news and instead using eye-catching headlines that included exaggerations of news events or sensationalism.


Every 'hyperlink' that appears on your Facebook newsfeed will be analyzed. After this an algorithm does a prediction if the content is a clickbait or not.
